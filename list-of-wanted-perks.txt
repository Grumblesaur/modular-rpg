Sense of Direction: Can use Intuition instead of Knowledge for Navigate checks,
  but cannot benefit from orienteering tools when using Intuition.

Nonverbal Intimidation: Can use Vigor instead of Presence for Intimidate
  checks.

Dueling Artist: Can use Finesse instead of Vigor when wielding a Melee weapon.

Gone Ballistic: Can use Vigor instead of Finesse when wielding a Thrown weapon.
